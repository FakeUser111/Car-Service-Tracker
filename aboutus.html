About Car Service Tracker

Welcome to Car Service Tracker, your ultimate car maintenance companion. Our platform ensures that you never miss a service date, keep track of your vehicle expenses, and connect with trusted mechanics effortlessly.

🚗 Our Mission

We aim to simplify car maintenance by providing a **smart, intuitive, and automated solution** for tracking vehicle services and expenses. Our goal is to help car owners **save time, money, and extend the life of their vehicles**.

🔧 Key Features

  • 📅 **Service Scheduling** – Track and plan car maintenance
  • 💰 **Expense Tracker** – Monitor fuel, repairs, and service costs
  • 🔍 **Mechanic Finder** – Locate and book reliable service centers
  • 📜 **Service History** – Access past maintenance records easily
  • 🌙 **Dark Mode & Custom UI** – Personalize your experience

⭐ Why Choose Us?

Unlike other car tracking solutions, we provide a **seamless and user-friendly experience** with real-time updates, cloud storage, and powerful features designed for every car owner.
