Car Servicing Guide - FAQ

1. What regular maintenance should I do?
  • Engine oil change – every 5,000 to 10,000 km
  • Brake inspection – every 10,000 km
  • Tire rotation and alignment – every 10,000 km
  • Battery check – every 6 months
  • Coolant & fluid levels – monthly
2. How do I know if my car needs servicing?
  • Unusual engine noise
  • Warning lights on dashboard
  • Reduced fuel efficiency
  • Brake issues or vibrations
3. Tips to increase car life?
  • Drive smoothly and avoid rapid acceleration
  • Check tire pressure regularly
  • Keep your car clean (including underbody)
  • Don’t ignore dashboard warning lights
